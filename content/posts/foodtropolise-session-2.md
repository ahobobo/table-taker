The group infiltrates a Pizzaria Arcade that is a front for the criminal organization known
as the Goudafellas. They successfully get everything they needed and more but are pushed to
the limit.

## Goudafellas

Angela drives the group to Goudafellas. Explaining that this innocent looking family arcade is
actually a front for an up and coming gang. They have a large cache of food that the could be
enough to convince the Don to not demolish their neighborhood. The group is tasked with getting in, 
getting the food and meeting back up with Angela in the back of the building.

The group enters an arcade with families playing games. The only visible staff is Greg the Capybara.
Ashe notices that he is a gunpla fan and starts talking with him. The rest of the group notices that
the highest ticket prize is a lunch with Chuck Goudafella himself. 

The group decides to earn enough tickets to get in peacefully. To do so, they play on an arm wrestling
machine. Whip tries to play but ends up getting flung by the machine. Landing on a pizza that was being
served by a robot. Cornelius takes a shot next and is easily able to get the high score.

With the tickets acquired, the group purchases their prize. Greg opens the door to the staff area and the
group meets the big cheese himself...

## Chuck Goudafella

Chuck and 3 goons where ready to receive the group. The rat was fat, greasy and crass. He immediately coming
on to Lulu, clearly having a thing for deers. After some brief chit chat he opened a large vault door and came
back with the first course of food. While he and his goons ate, Lulu passed the food over to Whip, who bravely
took the massive portions all by herself, keeping Lulu nimble. Ashe and Cornelius worked together to peak into
the now open vault. Corn blocked the view with his massive frame, Ashe peaked inside.

Ashe found the vault full of massive heavy crates of food. It was clear that getting this out in a sneaky matter
would be very difficult. With her scouting mission successful, she waited for Chuck to fetch the second course before
explaining the situation to the group.

## Chuck's Bargain

Chuck brings out the second course. Lulu once again lets Whip deal with the food, however at this point she was more
than a little stuffed. She would not be able to get through another course. Thankfully Chuck was talkative at this point,
making it clear that he would do just about anything to get at the Fat Cats. Sensing an opportunity, the group lied, saying
that they too where looking to get at the Fat Cats. 

Chuck is convinced and is willing to give the group his food stash after he has spiked it with a special concoction that
makes any animal go feral. The group further convinces him to let them have the food without it being spiked.

It looked like the group would be going home scot free. The food and a bonus feral serum in their hands. However, Chuck
had one final test...

## The Cheese Wheel

The back loading area of the building was blocked by a giant cheese wheel. Chuck had it there to ensure no one could
pass. Now he wanted the group to prove their metal by eating it. 

The group got to work. Unfortunately for them, the wheel proved to be the group's greatest challenge yet. Corn, Ashe and
Lulu all almost got knocked out by the shear amount of cheese they ate. Whip seemed to have been warmed up from the previous
stuffing and was able to break through the wall of cheese.

## Conclusion

With the food in hand, the group limped into Angela's car and headed back home. They made it out of Goudafellas in one piece.
Now they just need to get the food home. While riding in a clearly overloaded car. In a bad neighborhood. How bad could it be?
